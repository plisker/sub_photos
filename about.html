A little about me

I was gifted my first camera when I turned five. Since then, I've been fascinated by photography, not only for its power to transport us back to a specific memory, but also for its ability to immortalize ephemeral moments in time for others to enjoy.

After an unexpected encounter with a Great Gray Owl during my first hike in Yellowstone National Park, I became passionate about bird photography. Since then, I've had the privilege of spending countless hours in the field exploring nature, from the San Jacinto mountains in California to a rocky Atlantic Puffin colony off the coast of Maine—and beyond.

In looking through my photos, I hope you enjoy your journey through some of my most prized memories.
