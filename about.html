A little about me

I was gifted my first camera when I turned five. Since then, I've been fascinated by photography, not only for its power to transport us back to a specific memory, but also for the ability to immortalize ephemeral moments in time for others to enjoy.

After an unexpected encounter with a Great Gray Owl during my first hike in Yellowstone National Park, I became passionate in bird photography, a hobby that only strengthened during the pandemic. I hope you enjoy some of my memories.
